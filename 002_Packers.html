Let's Predict the rest of the Green Bay Packer's Season

Who are the 2020 Green Bay Packers

So far in the 2020 NFL season, the Green Bay Packers sit atop the NFC North Division with a record of 6-2. A record like this puts the Packers at the top of the Conference with other impressive teams like the Seahawks, Saints, and Bucs. Additionally, after a few down years, Aaron Rodgers, the Packer's quarterback, has seemed to play up to the standard that NFL fans worldwide know that Rodgers is capable of. He has firmly supplanted himself in the League MVP discussion, with the help of wide receiver Davante Adams and running back Aaron Jones. In short, up to this point, the Packers have had a very successful season.
